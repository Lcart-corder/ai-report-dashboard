<idea>
  **Design Movement**: Neumorphism 2.0 (Soft UI with Depth)
  **Core Principles**:
  1. **Tactile Realism**: Elements should feel like they can be touched, with soft shadows and highlights creating a sense of depth.
  2. **Calm & Focused**: A soothing color palette and minimal distractions to allow users to focus on complex data.
  3. **Fluidity**: Smooth transitions and rounded corners to create a seamless flow between sections.
  4. **Data as Art**: Visualizations should be beautiful and informative, integrating seamlessly into the soft UI.

  **Color Philosophy**:
  - **Base**: Soft, off-white or light gray backgrounds (e.g., #F0F2F5) to reduce eye strain.
  - **Accents**: Muted pastels (Sage Green, Dusty Blue) for status indicators and highlights, promoting a sense of calm control.
  - **Text**: Dark gray (not pure black) for readability without harsh contrast.
  - **Intent**: To reduce cognitive load for users dealing with dense analytics, making the dashboard feel approachable and less intimidating.

  **Layout Paradigm**:
  - **Card-Based Flow**: Content is organized into soft, floating cards that drift into place.
  - **Asymmetric Balance**: Avoid rigid grids; use whitespace to guide the eye naturally from summary to detail.
  - **Layered Depth**: Use z-index and shadow intensity to indicate hierarchy (e.g., active elements float higher).

  **Signature Elements**:
  - **Soft Shadows**: Double shadows (light and dark) to create the "extruded" look of Neumorphism.
  - **Frosted Glass**: Subtle glassmorphism effects on overlays and sticky headers.
  - **Rounded Everything**: Generous border-radius (16px-24px) on cards, buttons, and inputs.

  **Interaction Philosophy**:
  - **Press & Release**: Buttons should visually "press down" when clicked.
  - **Hover Lift**: Cards should gently lift up when hovered to invite interaction.
  - **Smooth Toggles**: Switches and toggles should slide with a satisfying, physical feel.

  **Animation**:
  - **Entrance**: Elements should fade in and float up gently (staggered).
  - **State Changes**: Morphing shapes rather than abrupt cuts.
  - **Micro-interactions**: Subtle pulses or glows on critical alerts.

  **Typography System**:
  - **Headings**: **Nunito** (Rounded sans-serif) for a friendly, approachable vibe.
  - **Body**: **Quicksand** or **Mulish** for high legibility and a modern, soft feel.
  - **Hierarchy**: Use font weight and color opacity rather than size alone to distinguish levels.
</idea>

<idea>
  **Design Movement**: Cyber-Corporate (Futuristic Professional)
  **Core Principles**:
  1. **Precision & Clarity**: Sharp lines, high contrast, and data-first presentation.
  2. **Tech-Forward**: A aesthetic that screams "AI" and "Advanced Analytics" without being cheesy sci-fi.
  3. **Efficiency**: Dense information density managed through smart collapsible sections and clear hierarchy.
  4. **Trust & Authority**: A look that inspires confidence in the AI's recommendations.

  **Color Philosophy**:
  - **Base**: Clean White (#FFFFFF) with very light cool gray sections.
  - **Accents**: Electric Blue (#2563EB) and Vibrant Teal (#0D9488) for primary actions and positive metrics.
  - **Text**: Deep Navy or Slate (#0F172A) for maximum contrast and authority.
  - **Intent**: To convey speed, accuracy, and cutting-edge technology. The "Official LINE" green should be integrated as a specific status color, not the dominant brand color, to maintain a "SaaS wrapper" feel.

  **Layout Paradigm**:
  - **Dashboard Grid**: A more structured, modular grid but with breaking elements (e.g., full-width headers).
  - **Sidebar Navigation**: A persistent, sleek sidebar that anchors the experience.
  - **Split Views**: Frequent use of split panes (list vs. detail) to manage complex data relationships.

  **Signature Elements**:
  - **Thin Borders**: 1px borders in cool gray to define structure.
  - **Tech Patterns**: Subtle circuit-board or dot-matrix patterns in empty states or headers.
  - **Monospace Accents**: Use a monospaced font for IDs, code snippets, or raw data values.

  **Interaction Philosophy**:
  - **Snappy Feedback**: Instant hover states and click responses. No lag.
  - **Data Drill-down**: Clicking a chart segment instantly filters the table below.
  - **Keyboard First**: Heavy focus on hotkeys and keyboard navigation (though implemented visually).

  **Animation**:
  - **Slide & Reveal**: Panels slide in from the side; details expand from within rows.
  - **Chart Growth**: Charts animate from zero to value quickly and smoothly.
  - **Loading States**: Skeleton screens with a shimmering "scan" effect.

  **Typography System**:
  - **Headings**: **Inter** (Tight tracking, bold weights) for a standard, clean tech look.
  - **Body**: **Inter** or **Roboto** for neutral readability.
  - **Data/Code**: **JetBrains Mono** or **Fira Code** for numbers and IDs.
</idea>

<idea>
  **Design Movement**: Swiss Style / International Typographic Style (Modernized)
  **Core Principles**:
  1. **Grid Systems**: A strict, mathematical grid that organizes content logically.
  2. **Asymmetry**: Dynamic, asymmetrical layouts that create visual interest while maintaining order.
  3. **Typography as Image**: Large, bold typography used as a primary design element.
  4. **Objectivity**: Clear, unadorned presentation of information.

  **Color Philosophy**:
  - **Base**: Stark White (#FFFFFF) canvas.
  - **Accents**: Bold, primary colors (Red, Blue, Yellow) used sparingly but effectively. For this specific context, we will adapt to the "LINE Green" (#06C755) as the primary accent, paired with a deep Swiss Red for alerts.
  - **Text**: Black (#000000) for maximum contrast.
  - **Intent**: To present the AI report as a factual, objective document of high importance.

  **Layout Paradigm**:
  - **Modular Grid**: Content blocks align perfectly to a visible or invisible grid.
  - **Whitespace**: Massive amounts of whitespace to frame the content and give it importance.
  - **Typographic Hierarchy**: Size and weight do all the heavy lifting for structure.

  **Signature Elements**:
  - **Heavy Rules**: Thick black or colored lines separating sections.
  - **Big Numbers**: Key metrics displayed in massive font sizes.
  - **Geometric Shapes**: Simple circles or squares used for icons or status indicators.

  **Interaction Philosophy**:
  - **Direct Manipulation**: Interactions feel direct and unmediated.
  - **Clear States**: Active, hover, and disabled states are unmistakably distinct.
  - **Minimal Motion**: Animations are rare and only used for essential transitions.

  **Animation**:
  - **Cut**: Instant changes for tabs or views (no fade).
  - **Slide**: Simple sliding transitions for drawers or modals.
  - **Type Reveal**: Text appearing line by line (fast).

  **Typography System**:
  - **Headings**: **Helvetica Now Display** (or **Arial** / **Roboto** as web safe fallback, but styled tightly) - Bold, tight tracking.
  - **Body**: **Helvetica Now Text** - Clean, neutral.
  - **Hierarchy**: Dramatic size contrast (e.g., 64px headings vs 14px body).
</idea>
